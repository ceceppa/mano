# Design Constraints

<!-- Project-wide design rules. Mia and Marco will respect these. -->

## General

- No gradients — use flat, solid colours only.
- No shadows heavier than subtle elevation.

## Mobile-specific

- Touch targets minimum 44x44px.
- No hover states — design for tap only.
- No horizontal scrolling.

## Colour

- WCAG AA minimum contrast (4.5:1 for text).

## Typography

- No custom web fonts requiring network loading. System or bundled fonts only.

## Animation

- Under 300ms. No decorative animations.
